About Me

With over 16 years of experience in financial services, I bring a unique blend of business acumen, data analysis, and UX-focused solutions. I’ve led cross-functional teams in Agile environments to deliver high-impact solutions across retail banking, insurance, and global operations.

My career began at Sun Life Financial where I supported internal systems and disaster recovery. I later joined Scotiabank, evolving from a Business Systems Analyst to a Business Analyst Specialist. There, I managed large-scale initiatives like Fraud Analytics redesign, AFT automation using Pega Robotics, and KYC/AML compliance. I used tools like SQL, Tableau, Visio, JIRA, and Pega to create data-driven and user-centered process improvements.

Most recently, I completed the Google Data Analytics Certificate, applying my learning in a data project for Bellabeat where I analyzed fitness tracker data and visualized insights using Tableau and BigQuery.

I’m passionate about continuous learning, lean thinking, and solving complex business problems through data and technology.
